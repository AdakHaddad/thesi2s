\textbf{Catatan koneksi PCM5102A:}
\begin{itemize}
\item \textbf{FLT} $\to$ GND (filter \textit{normal phase}).
\item \textbf{DEMP} $\to$ GND (\textit{de-emphasis} nonaktif).
\item \textbf{XSMT} $\to$ VCC (3,3\,V; aktifkan keluaran analog).
\item \textbf{VCC} (modul) $\to$ VCC 3,3\,V Pmod Basys3.
\item \textbf{GND} (modul) $\to$ GND Pmod Basys3.
\end{itemize}
